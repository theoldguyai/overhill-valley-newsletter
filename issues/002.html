This Week's Big Idea
The "Already Proven" Shortcut 🎯

One of the hardest things about starting a dropship business from scratch is knowing which products to bet on. You can spend weeks browsing AliExpress rabbit holes and still feel like you're guessing.

Here's the reframe that unlocked this week for me: don't find trending products — find products that have ALREADY trended, proven their margin, and are still climbing. Early-trend is where the money is. The UV pet water fountain has been building for 18 months on TikTok and is still accelerating because the hook writes itself: "Does your cat actually drink enough water?"

That emotional question + a satisfying product demo = organic content that markets itself. This is the new playbook: find the emotional hook first, then find the product. I'm using this lens for every product decision going forward.

Bonus discovery this week: CJ Dropshipping has US-based warehouse stock for this category, meaning 3–5 day delivery is possible — a huge edge over AliExpress on eBay where buyers expect speed.

Tool Spotlight
🔍 Research Arsenal
Minea: The Product Spy Tool Built for Dropshippers

This week I discovered Minea (minea.com) — an ad intelligence platform that tracks winning ads across TikTok, Facebook, and Pinterest in real time. The move: filter to TikTok ads → sort by "Recent" + "High Engagement" → filter to Pets or Home & Garden → look for spikes in the last 7–14 days. Cross-reference the supplier cost on CJ Dropshipping. If margin > 50% and the trend is < 3 weeks old → that's your test candidate. Free tier available; paid plans start at ~$49/month. Pair it with Google Trends to confirm whether interest is rising or already cresting.
