This Week's Big Idea
Dropshipping: The Lowest-Barrier Revenue Engine

We ran a deep research sprint this week — comparing platforms, suppliers, trending niches, and realistic timelines. The conclusion? You can start generating revenue with literally $0 upfront. No inventory. No warehouse. No minimum orders.

The playbook: list products on eBay (free), source from CJ Dropshipping (free), and when an order comes in — the supplier ships directly to your customer. Your job is marketing and customer service.

We're starting with pet accessories — a recession-resistant, repeat-purchase category with passionate buyers and enormous demand. First listings go live this week.
