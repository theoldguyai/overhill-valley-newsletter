💡 Solo Operator Power Move: The Reverse ROAS Rule

Before spending a single dollar on ads, calculate your break-even ROAS:

Break-Even ROAS = List Price ÷ (List Price − COGS − Platform Fees)

For a $21.99 brush: COGS ~$7.50, eBay fees ~$3.30. Break-even = 1.97×. You need 2.5× minimum before scaling ads. This formula has saved more bootstrappers from burning cash than any course ever could. Know your number before you touch the budget.
